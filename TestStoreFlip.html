Our Story

Miss Molly’s is a fully licensed family-run diner that has been serving the community for over 40 years! We serve all-day breakfast starting at 6:00 am on weekdays and 7:00 am on weekends.


Our lunch/dinner menu includes items like pizza, burgers, wings, salads & recently includes Lebanese cuisine such as shawarma sandwiches, platters & poutines, as well as desserts & appetizers.


We offer quick pick up and delivery off of our own online ordering system for our entire menu.


Please feel free to contact us via phone at (613)366-5548 for any inquiries regarding Miss Molly’s and we hope to see you soon!
